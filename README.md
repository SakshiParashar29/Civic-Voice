## What Problem Does CivicVoice Solve?

Traditional civic complaint reporting systems are often slow, fragmented, and lack proper tracking.  
CivicVoice solves this by providing:

- A central platform for users to submit complaints  
- Real-time complaint tracking  
- Upvoting to highlight urgent issues  
- Role-based dashboards (User + Admin)  
- Improved transparency & accountability in municipal services
